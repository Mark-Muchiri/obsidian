Features

  • Auto Toggle (Premium): Automatically enter windowed fullscreen mode whenever you go to a YouTube video. Search bar will appear while in windowed fullscreen mode if you mouse over where the bar usually is.
  • Scrolling Support (Premium): Now able to scroll the page like normal when in windowed fullscreen mode! Search bar will appear if you mouse over where it usually is, or scroll down a bit.
  • Hide Scrollbar whilst in Scrollable Mode (Premium): Enjoy a cleaner viewing experience with more visual space by hiding the scrollbar when scrolling.
  • Hide 'Includes Paid Promotion' Overlay on Thumbnails (Premium): Middle clickers rejoice! Hide the annoying 'Includes Paid Promotion' overlay on thumbnails on the Subscriptions page so you don't accidentally open the google support page on paid promotions.
  • Mini-player (Premium): While scrolling down the page, see a mini player so that you don't miss anything as you browse comments
  • Picture-in-Picture Shortcut (Premium): Quickly toggle Picture-in-Picture mode using Alt/Option + O, making it easy to watch videos while browsing other content.
  • Always Show Search Bar: Keep YouTube's search bar visible while in windowed fullscreen mode, making it easier to search for new videos without leaving fullscreen.
  • Show Video Title: Shows the video title when the mouse is moved on the player in windowed fullscreen mode, helping you keep track of what you're watching.
