Important Update: Moving to Lifetime Access for $12 AUD

Based on overwhelming user feedback and requests, we're making an important change to our premium access model. We're moving from yearly subscriptions to a one-time lifetime purchase of $12 AUD.

What's changing?

  • All existing yearly subscriptions will be automatically converted to lifetime access
  • Your recurring payments will be automatically cancelled
  • You'll maintain permanent access to all premium features

Thank You to Our Early Supporters!

To our existing subscribers: Thank you for your early support! While new users will pay $12 AUD for lifetime access, you're being automatically upgraded to lifetime access at no additional cost. This is our way of showing appreciation for your early support and trust in our extension.

Why This Change?

After receiving feedback from our users, it became clear that many prefer a one-time purchase over recurring payments. This change simplifies things for everyone and ensures you have permanent access to all premium features without worrying about renewals.

Although I understand that the initial yearly subscription was not a popular decision due to putting auto-toggle behind a paywall, I had to ensure that subscribers were getting the best value for their money. This should provide the best middle ground.

Update Notes

In addition to the pricing model change, we've made several technical improvements in this update:

  • New Premium Feature: Picture-in-Picture Shortcut - Quickly toggle Picture-in-Picture mode using Alt/Option + O, making it easy to watch videos while browsing other content.
  • New Feature: Always Show Search Bar - You can now keep YouTube's search bar visible while in windowed fullscreen mode, making it easier to search for new videos without leaving fullscreen.
  • New Feature: Show Video Title - Shows the video title when the mouse is moved on the player in windowed fullscreen mode, helping you keep track of what you're watching.
  • New Settings UI - Streamlined and optimised with a bit more polish!
  • Updated the extension to work with YouTube's latest layout changes
  • Improved reliability of the windowed fullscreen icon appearing in the player
  • Enhanced the auto-toggle functionality to work more consistently
  • Improved navigation handling with less issues going between pages
  • Overhauled the extension settings UI for a more modern and user-friendly experience

These changes are part of our ongoing commitment to keeping the extension working smoothly with YouTube's evolving interface.
